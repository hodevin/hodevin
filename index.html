About Me

Hello! I have a long list of experiences, so I hope you are able to value each individual experience as much as I do! Here's some background on me.
I am a first-generation college student looking to grow my network and interpersonal skills in the professional world.

I am a graduate of the class of 2021 at Allegheny College with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a minor in Economics.

I believe that we can harness technological advancements to improve an individual's well-being. I strongly advocate the significance of business strategy because I enjoy maximizing value for the consumer as well as the firm. I am interested in product management, UX/UI design, strategy, data analysis, business analysis, and QA engineering.

I am currently providing interpretation services from Cantonese to English and vice versa in court hearings as well as doctor's appointments. As a Test Automation Consultant, I have extensive experience designing, implementing, and maintaining automated testing systems for software applications. In addition to my technical skills, I have proven success as a Scrum Master, guiding cross-functional teams toward successful delivery of high-quality products.

My passion for technology and product development led me to explore further into the field of Product Management. I am eager to continue learning and growing in this area, leveraging my expertise in both software testing and project management to drive product success and make an impact on the industry. I enjoy staying up to date on the latest industry trends and exploring new technologies in my free time. I am also a firm believer in the importance of giving back and actively participating in volunteer work to support local communities.
